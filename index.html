What is a Hackathon? +

A hackathon is a collaborative event where teams come together to brainstorm, design, and build creative projects within a set timeframe based on the theme announced. For example, teams might create a mobile app, design a video game, build a website, or develop a hardware prototype!

What is the theme? +

The theme will be announced at the start of the event on Tuesday! All projects must incorporate the theme in some way.

Do I need coding experience to participate? +

Not at all! BrainJam welcomes participants of all skill levels.

How do teams work? +

Teams can have 2-5 members. You can come with a pre-formed team or find teammates at our team formation event on Tuesday (Day 1).

What should I bring? +

Bring your laptop, charger, and any hardware you want to hack with. We'll provide food, drinks, WiFi, and plenty of workspace.

Is there a cost to participate? +

BrainJam is completely free for all participants! Registration, meals, and swag are included at no cost.

How do I become a sponsor? +

We'd love to have you! Reach out to us at brainjam.hackathon@gmail.com or fill out the form below.

I have a specific question +

Feel free to email us at brainjam.hackathon@gmail.com or join our Discord server to ask questions and connect with other participants!
