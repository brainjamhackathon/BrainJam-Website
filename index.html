Frequently Asked Questions

✮
✮
How do I become a sponsor? +

We'd love to have you! Reach out to us at sponsor@brainjam.io or click the "Sponsor" button at the top of the page. We offer various sponsorship tiers with different benefits including logo placement, recruiting opportunities, and more.

Do I need coding experience to participate? +

Not at all! BrainJam welcomes participants of all skill levels. Whether you're a coder, designer, business mind, or just have great ideas—there's a place for you. We'll have workshops and mentors to help you learn along the way.

How do teams work? +

Teams can have up to 4 members. You can come with a pre-formed team or find teammates at our team formation event on Friday night. Solo participants are also welcome!

What should I bring? +

Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and any hardware you want to hack with. We'll provide food, drinks, WiFi, and plenty of workspace. A sleeping bag or blanket is optional if you plan to rest overnight.

Is there a cost to participate? +

BrainJam is completely free for all participants! Registration, meals, swag, and access to all workshops and activities are included at no cost.

Who can participate? +

BrainJam is open to all university students with a valid student ID. High school students (16+) may participate with parental consent. Recent graduates (within 1 year) are also welcome.
